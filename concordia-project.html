Concordia University Texas Website Redesign

Over the last two years I have been working with design and development companies to redesign the Concordia University Texas website. There were two major phases of the website redesign, the design and development of the templates and then the implementation of those templates into our web content management system (CMS) which is an XSLT-based CMS called Omni CMS provided by Modern Campus.

My role in this project was to follow each of the phases of the project, meet with stakeholders, vendors, developers, etc. and ensure the success of the project. I tested templates during both the design and implementation phases, made minor code changes, and many content updates.

From this project, I have learned the importance of being detail-oriented, staying on top of deadlines, and asking many questions. I also learned that it is essential to designate time to think about possible issues that could occur at each phase of the project and make measures to reduce risk.
